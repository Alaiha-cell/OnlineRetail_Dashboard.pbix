# 🛒 Online Retail Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

This dashboard visualizes online retail transactions to uncover sales trends, top-performing products, and country-wise revenue using Power BI.

---

## 🔍 Dataset Details
- Columns: InvoiceNo, StockCode, Description, Quantity, InvoiceDate, UnitPrice, CustomerID, Country
- Source: Online Retail Dataset (UCI Machine Learning Repository)
- Cleaned in Power BI and analyzed using DAX

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features
- KPI Cards: Total Sales, Total Orders, Quantity Sold, Unique Customers
- Monthly Sales Trend (Line Chart)
- Top 10 Products by Sales (Bar Chart)
- Sales by Country (Pie / Donut Chart)
- Sales by Country (Bar Chart)

---

## 💡 Key Insights
- UK generates the highest revenue overall.
- Products with high quantity don’t always have highest revenue — price also matters.
- Majority of sales revenue concentrated in top 5 countries.
- Sales have seasonal patterns over months.
